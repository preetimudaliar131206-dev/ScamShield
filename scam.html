Verify URLs. Fake sites mimic real bank domains.

Call your bank back using the number on your card.

📧 Email Safety Tips

Check the sender's email address carefully. Phishers use look-alike domains.

Banks rarely send attachments. Never open .zip or .exe from unknown emails.

Look for generic greetings like "Dear Customer" instead of your name.
